1. What is reverse-i-search in a Linux terminal?
A. Reverse-i-search (reverse incremental search) is a handy feature in Linux terminals that allows you to search for      and recall previously entered commands that match a specific pattern. It saves lot of time by finding and reusing the commands from the command history.
